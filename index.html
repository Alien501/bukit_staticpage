Our Story

It All Started with a Simple Idea!

Bukit was not born in an office boardroom or at a high-level strategy meeting. It started with a group of friends sitting in a cafe, casually brainstorming a desire to create something that could connect both entertainers and entertain lovers in an innovative way. We explored countless ideas, but ultimately, we saw a gap in the event ticketing platforms—too many clicks, missed opportunities and a lot of frustration for both event-goers and organizers.

And that’s when the idea for Bukit was born. A vision to bring simplicity and joy to event ticketing. Today, we have set our footprints in India and Singapore. But this is just a beginning. Our wings are ready to spread, and we are keeping our keen-eye on the UK, Europe, and beyond.
